My research endeavours have been on Style transfer for dialog response generation, Natural Language Understanding for agents, Suggestion Mining, Coreference Resolution, and Question answering.

Research

My research has focused on solving several semantic tasks in Natural Langauge Processing using Machine Learning algorithms.

Courteously Yours: Inducing courteous behavior in Customer Care responses using Reinforced Pointer Generator Network
Hitesh Golchha*, Mauajama Firdaus*, Asif Ekbal, Pushpak Bhattacharyya
NAACL-HLT ,2019

We develop a system that transforms a generic customer care response into a 'Courteous' one. The model takes into context the emotions and content of the conversation history as well while generating and is trained on MLE+RL.

Mauajama Firdaus, Hitesh Golchha, Asif Ekbal, Pushpak Bhattacharyya
